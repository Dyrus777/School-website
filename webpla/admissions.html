Our Application Process (Step-by-Step)

  1. **Inquire:** Begin by contacting our admissions office via phone or email to express your interest and ask any initial questions.
  2. **Download Application:** Download and complete our application form available below.
  3. **Submit Documents:** Gather all required documents (listed below) and submit them along with the completed application form to the school office during business hours.
  4. **Interview/Assessment:** We may schedule a brief informal interview with your child and parents to help us get to know your family better.
  5. **Enrollment Notification:** You will be notified of the admission decision by the specified deadline.
  6. **Registration & Tuition:** Upon acceptance, complete the registration process and submit initial tuition payments to secure your child's spot.

We encourage families to visit our school to experience our vibrant learning environment firsthand. Please contact us to schedule a tour!
